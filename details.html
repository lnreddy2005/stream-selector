Intermediate MPC

MPC stands for Maths, Physics, Chemistry. It is ideal for students who wish to pursue Engineering or other science-related degrees like B.Sc, Architecture, etc.

Intermediate BiPC

BiPC includes Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. This stream is best for careers in Medicine, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Biotechnology, and Life Sciences.

Intermediate CEC

CEC consists of Civics, Economics, and Commerce. It is suitable for careers in Business, Law, CA, CS, and Banking.

B.Tech CSE

Computer Science Engineering focuses on programming, software development, data structures, AI, and full-stack development.

B.Tech ECE

Electronics and Communication Engineering deals with electronics, signal processing, and embedded systems.

B.Tech Mechanical

Mechanical Engineering is the core field dealing with design, machines, thermal, and production industries.

Diploma Courses

Diploma courses are technical or skill-based short-term courses in areas like Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, and Computers.

CA / CMA

Chartered Accountant (CA) and Cost & Management Accountant (CMA) are professional commerce-based courses offering great scope in accounting and finance.

Design Courses

Design fields include Fashion Design, Graphic Design, UX/UI, Animation, and more. Creativity and portfolio-building are key here.
